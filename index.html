About

Who is aorysst

Aleksandar P.
Digital Artist & UI Designer

I am a problem solver, an art enthusiast, and a designer who is performing at the junction of design and crafting meaningful ideas.

Following my passion

Ever since I could remember, I have wanted to create. I brought to life my most challenging insecurities, passions, and most curious thoughts about the world. Today, I am still wondering about all the facets this universe has and continually finding new ways to understand who I am and what I am doing here. These thoughts influence my crafts directly and help me find myself. Constantly pondering, searching and creating.

Why design

As technology progresses things are getting more and more complex. So, I have a special opportunity to make these complex processes simple, delightful and joy to use every day. Design allows us to differentiate ourselves by showing attention and care about others. Everybody values design either consciously or unconsciously. I love creating thoughtful and meaningful experiences and make people happy by making things simple.

Process

How I work

    Strategy ➝ Ask first
    Before jumping into creating I always make sure that we’re asking the right questions and trying to accomplish the right challenges. This stage includes research, analyses, consulting and exploring possible solutions.
    Design ➝ Problem solving
    At the end of this phase you’ll have a pixel perfect design for your app or website. During the transition from wireframes into the final design I create prototypes simulating final end results before development.
    Development ➝ Bringing designs to life
    Since I’m a designer who can code too, I can easily work in close collaboration with developers up until the finish line and beyond. In case of web projects I also do coding myself.
    Quality assurance ➝ Transparent process
    Complete overview of my design process through regular meetings. After the initial design versions I’m happy to do user testing with customers to ensure the best possible solutions for their needs.
